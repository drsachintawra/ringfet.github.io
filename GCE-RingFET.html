GCE-RingFET

The cross-sectional view of GME-RingFET architecture and asymmetric doped RingFET i.e. CME RingFET is presented in Fig. 3.01 (a)-(d). In GME-RingFET, work-function of the gate near the drain side is lower than the source side (keeping doping inside the channel same). However, in CME RingFET, the doping of the channel near the source side is higher compared to drain side (having same work-function near the source and drain side). Both types of engineering techniques results in the modulation in threshold of the device i.e. higher near the source side compared to drain side region.

Figure 3.01. (a) 3D cross sectional view of GME-RingFET; (b) 3D cross sectional view of CME-RingFET; (c) showing the diagram of Gate Material Engineered device; (d) showing the diagram of Channel Material Engineered device [Kumar17b].
